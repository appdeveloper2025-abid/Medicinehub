Understanding Medicine Information

Generic vs Brand

Generic medicines contain the same active ingredients as brand-name drugs but are usually more affordable.

Side Effects

Most medicines can cause side effects. Always report unusual symptoms to your healthcare provider.

Drug Interactions

Some medicines can interact with each other, foods, or supplements. Always inform your doctor about all medications you're taking.

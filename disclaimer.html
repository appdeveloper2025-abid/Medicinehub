Medical Information Purpose

The information provided on Pharmadices is for educational and informational purposes only. The content is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Important: Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

This website serves as a reference tool for healthcare professionals, students, and patients to better understand medicines, but it should never replace personalized medical consultation.

Not Medical Advice

Pharmadices does not provide medical advice. The medicine information presented on this site should be used for reference purposes only. Your healthcare provider is the best source of information for questions and concerns about your medical condition and treatment.

Information categories that require professional interpretation include:

  • Drug uses and indications
  • Dosage recommendations
  • Side effects and adverse reactions
  • Drug interactions with other medications
  • Precautions and contraindications
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding safety

Prescription Medications

Information about prescription medications on this site is intended to enhance knowledge about medicines. This information should NOT be used:

  • To self-diagnose or self-treat medical conditions
  • As a substitute for a prescription from a licensed healthcare provider
  • To determine dosage without consulting a healthcare provider
  • To make decisions about starting or stopping any medication
  • To override or disregard professional medical advice

Warning: Self-medication can be dangerous and may lead to serious health complications, adverse drug reactions, or medication errors.

Accuracy of Information

  • Manufacturers may change product formulations without notice

  • Drug interactions can change as new drugs are introduced

Emergency Medical Situations

In case of a medical emergency:

Immediate Action Required

Do not use this website for emergency medical situations. Call your local emergency number immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.

Suspected Overdose or Adverse Reaction

If you suspect an overdose or are experiencing severe side effects from medication, seek emergency medical attention immediately.

Severe Allergic Reactions

For severe allergic reactions (difficulty breathing, swelling of face/lips/tongue), seek immediate medical help. Do not wait to consult this website.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the medicine information on PHARMADICES reliable?
Yes, our information is compiled from reputable medical sources. However, this is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.
Can I suggest adding a new medicine to the database?
Absolutely! Please contact us with the medicine details and reliable sources. We review all suggestions and add them if they meet our quality standards.
How often is the medicine database updated?
We regularly update our database with new medicines and revised information. Updates are typically made monthly or as new information becomes available.
Can I use PHARMADICES information for commercial purposes?
PHARMADICES is an educational project. For commercial use, please contact us to discuss licensing and proper attribution requirements.

IMPORTANT: PHARMADICES is for informational purposes only. For medical emergencies or urgent health concerns, contact your healthcare provider immediately.
